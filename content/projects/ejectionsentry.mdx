---


[![](/coro-angio2.png)]()

The heart's ejection fraction (EF) is an important metric for interventional cardiologists and other physicians that represent's,
in part, its health. Usually, the EF is obtained perioperatively by an ultrasound tech, however, this requires additional resources
and manpower. Ejectionsentry will address this problem by automatically determining the EF in angiograms.
